The Life and Art of Rosa Bonheur

Portrait of Rosa Bonheur by Edouard Louis Dubufe

Rosa Bonheur was a French female painter born in 1822. At the time of her death she was the most famous female painter in the world. Her most famous painting is The Horse Fair which resides in the Metropolitan Museum of Art. You can read more about her here.
